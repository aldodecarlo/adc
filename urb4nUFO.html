Project
urb4nUFO
Date
2013
Team Members
N. Giovannetti

Starting from the concept of loft, which basically consists in the renovation and revitalization of unusual crumbling spaces, the project sees the refunctionalisation of an aqueduct. Traditionally perceived as a non-liveable and completely blind object, the aim of urb4nUFO is to transform the structure as a great luminous open space, which gains the all possible solar irradiation thanks to the technological support of the FAKRO windows, translating the whole from a dark and blind space to a bright and perfectly human-conceived space.

Design

The project was designed for a competition in collaboration with the "Fakro" company that produces windows.
