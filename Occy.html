Project
Occy'
Date
2012

Design of a modular interactive session designed after winning a competition for the new Allegro Italiano hotel. The session was designed starting from the project logo. It can be made in different materials and can be connected to others in order to create combinations with two or three places close together or alternately to favor interaction between people..

Concept

Allegro Italiano Hotel studioata

"Our proposal is therefore to graft onto a repeatable and characterizing layout of the hotel chain. Which makes it possible to make the ITALIAN ALLEGRO brand recognizable, favoring the orientation and retention of the distinctive elements linked to the specific context and the surrounding territory, which go to make a decisive impact on the atmosphere of that specific hotel."
